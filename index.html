About

Hi, my name is Arbi, I'm 25 and I'm Italian. In my life I have always loved the beauty of the arts for this reason I first concentrated on design and then I discovered the world of the actor, now I have been dedicating myself to this beautiful world for 5 years. I define myself as a sunny boy, full of energy and always ready to discover new things. I speak three languages, Italian, Albanian and English, this is because I was born and raised in Italy, but my origins are Albanian, finally I learned English during my travels in the USA since part of my family is American. In addition to the arts, my passions include the world of comics and everything related to the world of nerds, riding my motorbike and doing martial arts (Brazilian Ju-Jitsu and Kung-Fu).
